Sree Vidyanikethan Engineering College (SVEC) is situated in lush green and scenic surroundings, 16 km from the famous temple-town of Tirupati. Established in 1996, it has over the years produced many brilliant engineering students who have made a mark for themselves and brought laurels to the Country. The College is affiliated to Jawaharlal Nehru Technological University (JNTU), Hyderabad, A.P. and is approved by the All India Council for Technical Education (AICTE), New Delhi. The College is accredited by National Board of Accreditation and accorded permanent affiliation by JNTU. After bifurcation of JNTU, Hyderabad the College is now affiliated to JNTU, Anantapur.
